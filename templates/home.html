What We Do

Our group creates a system linking "off the shelf" technologies in such a way that handwritten encoded messages can be digitized and processed by automated decryption techniques in order to decrypt them. The ideal goal of the project is for a group to demonstrate a working prototype of the entire “system” taking a handwritten encrypted message, digitizing it, and then using software to decrypt it.
